## Quick Intro

I'm super excited to share what I've been building - **OpenScope**! It's a visual plugin builder for Daydream Scope that lets you create video processing pipelines without writing any code.

## What It Does

Basically, you drag nodes onto a canvas, connect them together, tweak some sliders, and boom - you've got a custom video processor ready to export as a plugin!
